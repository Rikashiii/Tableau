Bar chart

3 types of bar chart
- simple
- side-by-side
- 100% stack


How to use the bar chart for decision making?
- Create horizontal bar chart of no. of orders by sub-category. Using color, identify the sub-categories where no. of orders are more than 3000



Side-by side bar chart
- This chart is used to compare one dimension values inside other dimension with measure. Number of bars created here are no. values in dimension 1 * no. of values in dimension 2.



100% stack bar chart-
- Same as side by side but the here one dimension values are stacked on top of other values in second dimension
