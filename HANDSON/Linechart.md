**Line chart**
used to represent data of time series.

to create line chat, use one date field and one measure.
alwys add the date field in column and easure in row for line chart.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Two varieties of line chart -

1. Discrete- created for individual date part. ex- hour day month quarter etc,
Shown in BLUE color

2. Continuos(TREND)- Here the line chart is created using year and individual date part month or quarter.
Shown in GREEN color.

Note- the color is not line color, its field color.
